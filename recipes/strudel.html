Wait for the cream!

Description

A strudel (/ˈstruːdəl/, German: [ˈʃtʁuːdl̩]) is a type of layered pastry with a filling that is usually sweet, but savoury fillings are also common. It became popular in the 18th century throughout the Habsburg Empire. Strudel is part of Austrian cuisine but is also common in other Central European cuisines.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • Egg

  2. Bake
  3. Wait for the cream!
